Veteran Services Culture Rebrand

Situation

Despite seeing a large increase in participation and engagement in Veteran Services events, Veteran Services staff observed that certain demographics of students and veterans remained un-engaged in events and services. The Veteran Services Program Manager tasked me with developing and executing a plan to increase the diversity of our engagement.

Tools

Surveys, Expert Opinions, Focus Groups, Event Planning & Coordination, Research, Data Analysis

Actions

  • Met with University demographics staff to discuss program objectives and design questions for a general engagement survey
  • Planned and facilitated focus groups for student-veterans and non student-veterans
  • Analyzed quantitative data regarding event attendance and facility utilization
  • Created and executed an engagement plan based on collected data

Result

Veteran services staff implemented an event strategy that increased engagement among female veterans, veterans of color, and veterans with children. The broadening of the event selection increased engagement on campus overall and ensured greater access to and awareness of available benefits for student veterans.

Document Compliance Automation

Situation

A compliance audit determined that our document compliance system was not scalable past one hundred clients and would need to be upgraded in order to maintain program compliance with Telecare Corporation’s Release of Information and documentation standards.

Tools

Smartsheet, Outlook, Technical Documentation Research, Stakeholder Engagement

Actions

  • Independently developed a user story for an automatic document expiry warning system
  • Collaborated with HART Program Manager and case management staff to define system requirements
  • Researched Smartsheet automation functions
  • Developed, implemented, and tested system functions
  • Held retrospectives and feedback sessions to gain feedback and continuously improve system function

Result

The Smartsheet automation system replaced a manual process of searching for expired releases of information with an automatic system that informed the relevant case management staff when their client’s ROI was within fourteen days of expiring. This solution satisfied our compliance department as it proved to be scalable far beyond our current caseload and enabled us to maintain a 95% document compliance rate.
